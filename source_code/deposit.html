deposit

Deposits are recorded as pending and are applied when you logout. Standard mode uses your logged-in name automatically. Admin must enter the holder name.

You’ll see a toast message like “deposit accepted” — and it will apply after logout.

How pending deposits work

• A deposit is stored as pending during the session.
• When you click logout, pending deposits are applied to the account.
• The account must be ENABLED.
• Admin must enter the exact holder name.
